Annemarie van Gaal

Annemarie van Gaal (The Netherlands, Helmond, 1962) is head of publishing company AM Media, a writer and columnist. She started her career at publishing company VNU, first as a secretary, later as a project manager.

In 1990 she went to Moscow and started Independent Media with publisher Derk Sauer. Among its twenty titles were the Russian language versions of Marie Claire and Cosmopolitan.
